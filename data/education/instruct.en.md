# Instructions for working with the system

## Welcome to HR PRO AI — the online personnel assessment system

To select productive employees, use the pairing of the **"Result"** and **"Tools"** tests: they assess a candidate's productivity and personal qualities.

Instead of reading a résumé, immediately send the applicant the **"Result"** performance test. It is more important for us to understand what real, measurable result a person achieves at work than how good they are at writing a résumé.

Having received and correctly assessed "Result", send **"Tools"** — it will reveal the personality traits the candidate uses to achieve results, and will help you assemble a harmonious team and avoid hiring an obviously unsuitable employee.

To select sales managers, use **"Sales"** — it reveals how well the candidate understands sales and which type of sales they are predisposed to. For executives and key roles — **"Logic"**: it assesses intelligence and the ability to make sound decisions quickly.

> **The main rule.** We hire based on productivity, and based on personal qualities we assess risk rather than filter out effective people. Let your company's history contain only suitable employees.

## Order of work

1. Create a **section** and a **vacancy** (for the vacancy, choose the language of the emails sent to candidates).
2. Send the candidate the **"Result"** test — this is the first productivity filter.
3. Correctly assess the answers and, if the candidate fits, send **"Tools"**.
4. Read the ready-made chart with its interpretation, syndromes, and AI hint.
5. Assign the candidate a stage (Interview, Reserve, Accepted, Rejection) — directly from the report.

## Call script before the first test ("Result")

Your voice on the call should be pleasant and engaging.

> Good afternoon, [Name]! My name is [ … ], I am the HR manager of the company [ … ]. Is it convenient for you to talk right now? — Yes. — You applied for our vacancy [ … ], and your résumé interests us. Before the interview we have two small stages — a questionnaire and a test. Please complete them today so that tomorrow we can arrange a meeting with the manager. The first test has already been sent to your email; if it hasn't arrived — call me back on this number and I'll resend it. Agreed? — Yes. — Thank you for applying, we look forward to your reply.

## Call script before the second test ("Tools")

> Good afternoon, [Name]! This is [ … ], the HR manager of the company [ … ]. We received your questionnaire for the vacancy [ … ], your results impressed us greatly, and we've sent the final test — it's already waiting in your inbox. We look forward to your reply.
